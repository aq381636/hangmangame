Rules For Hangman Game

Their are 6 diffenrent levels and 3 types of difficulty for this Game
You will be shown a number of dashes corresponding to the number of letters a word contains.

Players will guess letters they think the word contains.

Correct Guess: If the letter the players guesses is a letter in the word ,

then that letter will appear in the postion it is located in the word.

Wrong Guess: If the letter the players guesses is not a letter in the word ,

then a part of the hangmen appear.

Win or Loss

A player wins if they correctly guess all letters of a word before the hangmen drawing is complete.

A player loses if they guess incorrectly too many times and the

hangmen drawing is complete before they could guess all letters of the word.

The player wins if they guess the word before the hangman is complete.

The game can also end if the hangman is complete, meaning the player has used up all their guesses.
